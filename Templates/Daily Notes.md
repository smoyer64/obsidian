1. **The Top Section:** This is your **Properties** (Front-matter). It will render as a clean table at the top of your note.
2. **The Symbols:** * `- [ ]` creates a checkbox (Task).

    * `- <` represents a Migrated/Scheduled item.
3. **The Callout:** The `> [!ABSTRACT]` section creates a styled box for your messy thoughts so they don't clutter your clean list of tasks.
